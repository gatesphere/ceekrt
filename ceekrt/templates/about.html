What is ceekrt? ceekrt is a new idea in the world of microblogging. ceekrt allows you to anonymously contribute your secrets to a microblog, as a form of release and healing. Think of ceekert as PostSecret meets twitter. No names, no shame, just release.

The idea is simple: to post a secret, simply type it into the share your secret form, making sure to stay 140 characters or less--the bar will stop you if you try to type too many, and click submit. That's it!

See a post that is malicious in intent (attempts to inject remote code, or to spam the site, etc.), or isn't fitting with the feeling of the community? There's a report button next to each secret for that reason. But please, don't report secrets over-zealously because you are offended or don't agree with the content. This is an open community that relies upon the good judgment of its members.

ceekrt is maintained by its author, Jacob Peck, and is completely a hobby project. Occasionally I post a secret or two of my own here.

ceekrt is not affiliated with PostSecret or twitter in any way, and uses those names without permission.
